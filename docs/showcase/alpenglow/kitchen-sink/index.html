Disclosure

What is the return policy?

We offer a 60-day no-questions-asked return policy on all unused gear. Items must be in original packaging with tags attached. Used gear may be returned within 30 days if it has a manufacturing defect — we will repair, replace, or refund at your choice.

How do I care for my gear?

Wash shelters and packs with mild soap and lukewarm water — never use a washing machine. Hang dry in shade. Re-apply DWR coating after every 20 days of use or when water stops beading on the fabric. Store gear loosely in a cool, dry place; avoid compression sacks for long-term storage.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to over 40 countries via tracked international post. Delivery times range from 7–21 business days depending on the destination. Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the buyer. Free international shipping on orders over $250.

What's your warranty?

Every Alpenglow product carries a lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects. Normal wear and trail damage are not covered, but our repair shop can fix most issues at cost. Warranty claims can be submitted online with photos — most are processed within 48 hours.
