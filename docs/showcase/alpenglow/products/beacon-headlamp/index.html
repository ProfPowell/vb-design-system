Precision Light for Serious Adventures

The Beacon Pro uses an aspherical polycarbonate lens that produces a clean, focused beam with no dark spots or rings. At 700 lumens on high, it throws a usable beam to 150 meters — enough to navigate technical terrain at speed. The medium and low modes are tuned for camp tasks and extended-duration use, delivering 12 and 45 hours respectively from a single charge.

The 3350mAh lithium-ion battery charges fully in 2 hours via USB-C, and a four-LED indicator shows remaining capacity at a glance. The lock mode prevents accidental activation in your pack — a small detail that saves real frustration on multi-day trips. Press and hold both buttons for 3 seconds to engage or disengage the lock.

The red night-vision mode was designed with input from alpine climbers and astronomers. It provides enough light to read a map or find gear in your tent without destroying dark-adapted vision. The IPX8 waterproof rating means full submersion to 2 meters for 30 minutes — tested and certified, not just marketing. At 85 grams including the battery, the Beacon Pro disappears on your head during all-night pushes.

Mode Guide

Cycle through modes with a single press of the power button. The Beacon Pro remembers your last-used white mode and returns to it on the next power-on. Red mode is accessed via a dedicated second button.
