Our lightest full-featured pack. 32 liters of waterproof capacity with a hydration-compatible design, all at just 680 grams. Built for fast-and-light missions on technical terrain.

Free shipping on all orders. Lifetime repair promise included.

Engineered for the Long Trail

The Trail Runner Pack 32L was born from a single question: how light can a full-featured pack get without sacrificing durability? After three years of prototyping and over 2,000 miles of trail testing, we arrived at 680 grams of pure alpine capability.

The roll-top closure system provides infinite volume adjustment while maintaining a watertight seal rated to IPX4. Inside, a removable aluminum stay gives the pack structure under load, while the 3D air mesh back panel keeps you cool during high-output efforts. The hydration routing accommodates reservoirs up to 3 liters, with a magnetic hose clip integrated into the shoulder strap.

Every detail serves a purpose. The side mesh pockets stretch to hold 1-liter bottles securely. The hip belt is padded enough for comfort on long approaches but removable for scrambling or when you want to shave another 60 grams. The front shove-it pocket holds a rain shell or wet layers, and the dual ice axe loops are reinforced for real alpine use, not just trail-runner decoration.
